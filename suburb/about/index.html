About Flattie

We built Flattie because the Auckland housing market is in a chronic state of chaos. Its unaffordable. Fewer and fewer kiwis are able to afford a home and this is projected to get worse within the immediate future.

While an app like Flattie can't fix the housing crisis, we try to help people find affordable rentals with the app.

It's a start.

We hope you find Flattie useful. If you did, feel free to drop any of us a line.
