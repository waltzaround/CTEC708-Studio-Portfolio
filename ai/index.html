ChatFace

A barely-functional Messenger Bot that tries to perform place recognition.
It is cobbled together for an AI paper from various tutorials.

Machine learning with wit.ai

ChatFace relies on wit.ai for place and country recognition. It also relies on wit to understand the intent behind the text that ChatFace receives.

Built with the Messenger SDK

ChatFace is built with the Messenger SDK. This enables it to work on Facebook Messenger and anyone who has a Facebook account.

Hosted with Heroku

ChatFace is hosted on a service called Heroku. This enables it to scale as the amount of traffic to it fluctuates.
